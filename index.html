Health Information

Important Health Tips

Simple habits that keep you healthy every day.

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration improves digestion, skin health, and energy levels throughout the day.

Take Medicines on Time

Always take prescribed medicines at the correct time and dosage. Never skip doses without consulting your doctor first.

Balanced Diet

Eat fruits, vegetables, proteins and whole grains daily. A balanced diet boosts immunity and prevents chronic diseases.

Exercise Regularly

At least 30 minutes of physical activity daily reduces risk of heart disease, diabetes and improves mental health.

Quality Sleep

7-9 hours of sleep per night is essential. Good sleep improves memory, mood and overall physical health.

Hand Hygiene

Wash hands regularly with soap for at least 20 seconds. This simple habit prevents spread of infections and diseases.
